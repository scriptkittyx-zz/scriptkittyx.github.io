How Much Will I Be Billed?

We bill only for what the system produces each month at 20% discount from the reference rate.

SunStreet estimates the rate that otherwise would be paid to the local utility (the “Reference Rate”) by using: (i)the projected consumption of electricity of the home, (ii) the utility’s tiered rate structure, and (iii) other charges and taxes billed by the utility. The SunStreet Rate is guaranteed to be 20% less than the Reference Rate.
